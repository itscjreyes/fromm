The FROMM Group

Established in 1947, FROMM is a market-leading manufacturer of automated packaging solutions, wrapping machines, strapping machines, the patented Airpad pillow system, as well as all necessary consumables. We design, develop, and produce a wide variety of systems for unitizing and palletizing goods for transport.

We work closely with customers to develop custom packaging solutions that meet their requirements and enable them to reduce waste, cut costs, and increase operational efficiency.

FROMM is a Swiss family business with over 900 employees operating in more than 50 countries. We have more than 30 subsidiaries in North and South America, Europe, South Africa, and Asia, along with specialized sales partners throughout the world.

The FROMM Advantage

We offer all clients the FROMM advantage at no additional cost. Our aim is to deliver excellent solutions, service, and support to ensure your products arrive at their destination safely and in one piece.
